Our Expertise

Leading Through
Technical Excellence

FME is a recognized leader in mobility standards and technical consulting, with a focus on inclusive and future-ready engineering.

TGSI Standards Leadership

FME has been at the forefront of developing national standards for Tactile Ground Surface Indicators (TGSIs). Our leadership in the development of SANS 784:2022 ensures that infrastructure is safe and accessible for the visually impaired.

Project Capabilities

Our team has extensive experience in managing complex infrastructure projects, from initial feasibility through to construction and operational readiness. We specialize in BRT systems, urban precincts, and multi-modal transport hubs.
