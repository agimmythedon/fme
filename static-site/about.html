Leadership

Frano Combrinck Director & Lead Consultant

Frano Combrinck is a highly accomplished professional with over 15 years of experience in the transport and infrastructure sectors. His career is marked by a deep commitment to universal access and sustainable mobility.

He has played a pivotal role in developing national standards, including the SANS 784:2022 for Tactile Ground Surface Indicators (TGSIs), and has led major infrastructure projects across Southern Africa.

Under his leadership, FME has become a trusted partner for government agencies and private developers alike, delivering projects that prioritize safety, accessibility, and long-term viability.
